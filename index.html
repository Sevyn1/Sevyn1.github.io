Pipeline Montioring Solutions

Corrosion is one of the leading causes of failures in offshore and onshore transmission pipelines.

The Challenge

Pipeline corrosion is a major threat to oil and gas distribution services. This may negatively affect the supply chain creating problems for both clients and distributors alike. And that is where we come in.

Our Solution

Our Xdetect System is a great solution to the trials of pipeline corrosion. Using state of the art laser technology, the device can measure the amount of surface change in a pipline caused by corrosion. It is also excellent for long distances and difficult-to-reach locations.
